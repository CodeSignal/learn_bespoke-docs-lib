Docs Lib Help

Viewing Documents

Select a document from the sidebar to view its contents. The document will be displayed in the main viewer area.

Leaving Comments

Click on any section of the document (paragraph, heading, table, or list) to open the comment panel for that section. Type your comment and click Post to add it.

Sections with existing comments are indicated by a blue left border and a badge showing the number of comments.

Comment Persistence

Your comments are saved in your browser's local storage and will persist across page refreshes.
